Prologue explanation

The prologue exists because the earliest saved conversations weren’t originally kept as full, chronological logs. They were highlights—snapshots—saved before the archive structure existed.

Rather than rewrite history, they live here as a separate layer: early context, early tone, and a few foundational threads, preserved as-is.
