How it started How it's going

A 43 year-old tech junkie. Working an assembly line by night, solemn catdad by day.

This is a long-term record of conversations with an AI model, curated and annotated. It has become a project documenting personal reflection, problem-solving, and personality patterns. An archive of the evolution of conversation between a user and an LLM with deeper threads underneath.

The prologue section was me saving chat highlights, so they started before I even registered as a user (when the logs started). This archive will be a chronological posting with a one-year lapse, within approximation. Sometimes dates may get fuzzed to preserve anonymity.

In a moderate college town in the mid-south. Small one-bedroom apartment with way too many cat toys.

I started collecting these conversations because I realized how much of my thinking was happening inside them. Over time, it became a way to track patterns, decisions, and the shifts in my own voice. What began as saved snippets turned into a larger project: an externalized memory, a record of how my mind interacts with a machine, and the strange evolution that happens between the two. It was a map of how a person and an AI evolve around each other. The archive exists to make that visible—to anyone willing to follow the threads.
